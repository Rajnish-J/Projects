Project - Rock Paper Scissors
Let us build a simple Rock, Paper & Scissors game.

Play around with the game in the console to understand how it functions - you will create this yourself in this module.

Rules of the game explained

In each chance - you have to select one of Rock, Paper or Scissors
The computer also randomly selects one of Rock, Paper or Scissors
Who wins the round?
Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors).
Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper).
Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock).
If both select the same, then the round is a tie
The match is a best of 3 rounds.
